Guidelines

Submission Guidelines & Review Process

Follow these guidelines to ensure a smooth, efficient review of your project plans.

Residential

  • Swimming Pools
  • Solar Projects
  • Single Family Residential
  • Additions & Remodeling
  • Carport to Garage Conversion
  • New Patio Additions
  • Detached Accessory Structures

Commercial

  • New Ground-Up Construction
  • Tenant Improvements
  • Signs & Banners
  • Certificate of Occupancy (No Work)
  • Fire Alarms & AFES
1

All construction documents should be submitted in PDF format as a complete set.

2

Supplementary documents must be submitted separately using standardized naming conventions.

3

TrueType fonts are required for plan sets. Recommended: Arial, Times New Roman, Courier, Helvetica.

4

Avoid using red ink in any drawings or documents submitted.

Your submission must include a cover sheet summarizing:

Project Title & Description
Project Location
Applicant / Owner Details
Project Scope Overview
1

Your submission will be reviewed concurrently by relevant departments.

2

If revisions are required, you will receive a marked-up package with correction instructions.

3

After revisions, upload a complete updated set for further review.

4

Upon approval, you will be contacted regarding permit payment, and final documents delivered via email.
