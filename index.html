The Engine

Seamless Agentic Review

Purpose-built for building departments, architects, and contractors who need accurate compliance at scale.

1

Multi-Format Ingestion

Upload PDF, DXF, DOCX, or DWG. Our in-browser parser extracts text, geometry, and layer data — no server upload required.

2

Deterministic Rule Engine

Versioned JSON rule packs for IBC 2021, ADA 2010, and NFPA 101. Every finding carries the exact code section and remediation text.

3

Actionable Output

Printable compliance report, correction letter download, JSON export, and an AI chat panel to ask follow-up questions — all client-side.

100% private. All parsing and analysis runs entirely in your browser. No plan data is uploaded to any server unless you explicitly use the BYO-key LLM feature.

Guidelines

Submission Guidelines & Review Process

Follow these guidelines to ensure a smooth, efficient review of your project plans.

Residential

  • Swimming Pools
  • Solar Projects
  • Single Family Residential
  • Additions & Remodeling
  • Carport to Garage Conversion
  • New Patio Additions
  • Detached Accessory Structures

Commercial

  • New Ground-Up Construction
  • Tenant Improvements
  • Signs & Banners
  • Certificate of Occupancy
  • Fire Alarms & AFES
1

Construction documents submitted as a complete set in PDF format (or DXF/DOCX for automated review).

2

Supplementary documents submitted separately using standardized naming conventions.

3

TrueType fonts required for plan sets: Arial, Times New Roman, Courier, Helvetica.

4

Avoid red ink in any drawings or documents submitted for review.

1

Submission reviewed concurrently by relevant departments.

2

If revisions required, receive a marked-up package with correction instructions.

3

After revisions, upload a complete updated set for further review.

4

Upon approval, contacted regarding permit payment; final documents delivered via email.
